Color Scheme

I will be using a 5-color scheme, although 2 of the colors will only be used as small accents throughout the page.


The colors I plan to use are navy, a lighter version of navy, white, grey, and yellow.








Where will they be used?

The navy will be on the background of the header and footer, with the light navy on the navigation bar. It will also be used on the navigation bar when it is hovered.

I haven't quite yet decided on the color for the article text boxes, but it will likely be the lighter grey.

The off-white color will be used for text on the the darker backgrounds, such as the titles and navigation words. I will also use it for the hover color on the navigation boxes.

The yellow is the accent on the front page on the word "home."
