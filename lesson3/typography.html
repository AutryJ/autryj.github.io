Fonts

The fonts I chose to use are Merriweather and Raleway. I got them off of Google Fonts. I thought two fonts were enough to show contrast and variation, but not be overbearing with too many different styles.








Where do I use them?

Merriweather, a serif font, is used for the titles of the page. I have assigned it to the h1 tag. Raleway, a sans-serif font, is being used for all body text. I have also assigned Raleway to be the h2 tag, but I will also be bolding those headings.
